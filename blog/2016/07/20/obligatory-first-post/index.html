Obligatory First Post

20th July, 2016

So, I started writing a blog. This means that I need to write one of those obligatory first blog posts where I tell you about me and why I started blogging. As I am lazy, here is the text from my about page:

Who are you?

I’m Andy Oldroyd, a Software Engineer from Cambridge in the UK. I focus on testing and automation in my day job, but I also have interests in AI, machine learning and virtual reality.

I work mostly with JavaScript (both in the browser and server side with Node) and C#, so that is what the majority of what my programming posts will be about. I’m open to trying most languages though, particularly if it has an awesome library I want to use.

Why are you writing this blog?

I want a place to keep track of my side projects and experiments in a semi-organised fashion. You’ll find my research and opinions in the form of blog posts, some of which may even be interesting.

How can I contact you?

Send me an email at: contact@oldroyd.me

If you are in the Cambridge area then I also host the Cambridge Virtual Reality Meetup and attend other meetups in the area. There is a good chance you can see me at one of those.
