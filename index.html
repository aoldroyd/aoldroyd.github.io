About

I am a software engineer based in Cambridge, specializing in the successful delivery of innovative technologies within enterprise settings, particularly in regulated environments.

My experience extends beyond traditional software engineering, encompassing insights, guidance, and strategic input for the implementation of new and emerging technologies across diverse business functions. My hands-on experience includes directly implementing numerous diverse technologies to develop Proof of Concepts, ensuring that the insights I provide are not only strategic but also grounded in practical understanding. I collaborate and form partnerships with external companies from small startups to the tech giants to understand where technologies are going, and what new skills and capabilities are going to be needed to maximise the impact and value of them within an organisation.

I'm passionate about accelerating the adoption of innovative technologies, which means more than just understanding the benefits within the context of a proof of concept. I am capable of effectively communicating with strategic leaders, explaining the value of these technologies, and articulating potential risks to drive successful and long-lasting value.

Recent Projects

  • XR Projects
    Be it Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality or Spatial computing, eXtended Reality is starting to become a focus within the enterprise. I've worked on numerous projects from leveraging VR for training and building muscle memory with no risk to real equipment, through to the future of work collaboration and metaverse solutions.
  • Digital Twins
    Digital Twins are gaining popularity for their potential to enhance efficiency and revolutionize operational processes. My recent projects go beyond creating a 3D visual twin with basic predictions. Instead, they explore innovative approaches to represent underlying data, aiming to develop holistic twins capable of autonomously managing future facilities given nothing but natural language prompts and context from real-time data.
  • Generative AI
    Generative AI is currently one of the most discussed technologies. In recent projects, I've concentrated on expanding its applications beyond the established domains of text and images, exploring the future possibilities of this innovative technology.
  • Portal Development
    Having insights and knowledge about new and emerging technologies is valuable, but without awareness, the impact of these insights is limited. I've been instrumental in developing a polished website designed to effectively deliver these insights to key strategic leaders.
